Quick overview

Generative AI (GenAI) refers to models that can produce new content — text, images, code, audio, and video — trained on large datasets. This site explains how GenAI works, its strengths and limitations, and how to use it ethically in academic and daily contexts.

Who is this for?

Students, educators, researchers, developers, and curious learners who want clear guidance and practical rules-of-thumb for using GenAI responsibly.
